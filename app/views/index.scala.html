The next generation of usability and security

Security

We use provably secure Searchable Symmetric Encryption with industry standard 256 bit AES to ensure that your data is completely protected while still fully searchable.

Scalability

Our encryption scheme is massively scalable with little overhead, so it doesn't matter whether you have hundreds or millions of images. Your data will always be efficient and fast to retrieve.

Deep Learning

We use convolutional neural nets in order to process all your data locally on your device and we never use any third party APIs, so there is no chance of any your data will be leaked.

We take your security seriously. We make sure that only you have access to your secret key, and that only you can decrypt and view your data even in the case of your device being compromised.

Forget the security apps that require weeks of training to use correctly. We offer an intuitive UI based on your favorite apps, so you can jump right into protecting your data using the best encryption the industry has to offer.

We use the latest image recognition technology optimized for mobile devices in order to generate tags for your images. Our deep convolutional neural net can recognize 1000 different types of objects, making your experience much smoother.

Once you've uploaded your pictures, simply search by their tags and retrieve them instantly. The entire process is end to end encrypted, and no one gets to see your pictures or your searches except for you.
